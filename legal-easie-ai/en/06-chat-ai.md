# Chat AI (Web)

## Start a conversation

1. Go to **Chat**.
2. Create a new chat.
3. Ask your legal question.

## Best practices

- Provide case context when relevant.
- Ask clear and specific questions.
- Review legal references before making decisions.

## Limits

- AI message limits depend on your plan.
- Usage is visible in the chat sidebar.
