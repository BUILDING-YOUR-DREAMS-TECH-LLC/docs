# Documents

## Upload a document

1. Go to **Documents**.
2. Click **Upload Document**.
3. Select a file or drag and drop.
4. Add title, description, and type.
5. Save.

## Associate with client or case

- During upload, choose a related client or case.

## Download

- Open the document detail and click **Download**.
- A signed link is generated for secure access.

## Limits

- Storage limits depend on your plan.
