# Troubleshooting

## Cannot log in

- Verify email and password.
- Use **/auth/forgot-password** if needed.

## Cannot upload documents

- Check storage limits.
- Verify file format.
- Rename the file and retry.

## Google Calendar not connecting

- Re-authorize in **Settings > Profile**.

## WhatsApp not responding

- Confirm the phone number is registered.
- Check your plan limits.

## AI responses incomplete

- Ask more specific questions.
- Ensure the legal corpus is loaded by the admin.
