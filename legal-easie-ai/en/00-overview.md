## Purpose

This manual set explains how to use Legal Easie AI as a legal practice management platform with AI assistance.

## Modules at a glance

- Dashboard: high level metrics and quick actions.
- Clients: contact and identification records.
- Cases: legal matters, status, and priority.
- Documents: secure uploads and organization.
- Chat AI: web-based legal assistant.
- WhatsApp Assistant: mobile access to the assistant.
- Calendar: events and Google Calendar sync.
- Billing: plans, limits, and subscription management.
- Members & Roles: invitations and permissions.
- Settings: profile and organization preferences.
- Legal Base (Admin): global legal corpus for RAG.

## Recommended flow

1. Complete registration and profile setup.
2. Connect Google Calendar (optional).
3. Create clients and cases.
4. Upload documents.
5. Use Chat AI and WhatsApp for quick queries.
6. Review billing limits and upgrade if needed.

## Notes

- The assistant uses the global legal corpus uploaded by the admin.
- Plan limits apply to messages, storage, and team size.
