# Getting Started

## Registration

1. Go to **/auth/register**.
2. Enter email, password, full name, and organization name.
3. Submit the form and access the dashboard.

## Login

1. Go to **/auth/login**.
2. Use email/password or OAuth (Google/Microsoft).

## Email verification

- You may be asked to verify your email for sensitive actions.

## Initial setup checklist

- Update your profile in **Settings > Profile**.
- Connect Google Calendar if you want scheduling.
- Review your plan limits in **Billing**.

## WhatsApp (optional)

- Ensure your WhatsApp number is registered in your profile.
- The assistant recognizes users by phone number.
