# Members and Roles

## Invite members

1. Go to **Settings > Members**.
2. Click **Invite Member**.
3. Enter email and role.
4. Send invitation.

## Roles

- Owner: full control.
- Admin: manage data and members (no owner changes).
- Member: daily operations.
- Viewer: read-only.

## Limits

- Team size depends on your plan.

## Cancel invitations

- Use **Cancel** in the pending invitations list.
