# WhatsApp Assistant

## Activation

1. Ensure your WhatsApp number is registered.
2. Send a message to the configured WhatsApp number.
3. The assistant links your account by phone number.

## Queries

- Ask about legislation, cases, or your calendar.
- Clear messages improve results.

## Limits

- Usage counts toward your monthly AI limits.
- New users may have a limited trial.

## Security

- Avoid sharing highly sensitive data in chat.
- Use the web app for confidential documents.
