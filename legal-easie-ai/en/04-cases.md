# Cases

## Create a case

1. Go to **Cases**.
2. Click **New Case**.
3. Select a client.
4. Set title, status, and priority.
5. Save.

## Status and priority

- Status: open, in progress, closed, archived.
- Priority: low, medium, high, urgent.

## Case details

- Update description, court, judge, and notes for full context.

## Limits

- Case limits depend on your plan.
